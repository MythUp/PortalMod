Welcome to **PortalMod**, a project aiming to **reimagine the Portal experience** in Minecraft. 

Besides the **Portal Gun**, PortalMod adds a variety of **features from the Portal franchise, redesigned** to feel at home in Minecraft.

The mod is developed with **full survival compatibility** and **multiplayer support** in mind, for building and solving test chambers with friends. Cake included!

<br>

![](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/c0fbbf79fd11a2b47b0fca7481caf73cfe7f4eb6.png)

<img align="right" width="300" alt="Turret" src="https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/21e15c3f0e682732db03ca47a151225d98491fa1.png">

![Features](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/96acf62bd1de822cef04fdb7ff50563088726f64.png)

> This mod adds a **variety of test elements from the Portal games**, allowing you to create **your own test chambers**. 

> We got **Cubes** and **Cube Droppers** as well as cool **Buttons** for revolutionary cube-and-button based testing. These buttons connect to **Chamber Doors** with **Antlines**, similar to redstone.

> You can further enrich your chambers with **Gels**, **Fizzlers**, **Aerial Faith Plates**, **Autoportals** and more! Or add hazards like **Turrets** or **Deadly Goo** to remove unwanted test subjects after testing.

<img align="left" width="285" alt="Black and White panel blocks" src="https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/1b419f2e0b4c15564d059e74f57551cf344a23df.png">

We also, of course, didn't forget to include some awesome decorative blocks. The **white and black panels** come in **many variants**, as well as a lot of additional **variety in placement**. 

Decorate more with flickering **Chamber Lights**, configurable **Chamber Signs**, **Platforms** and **Beams** great for stairs, and some really nice types of **Iron Frames** for all kinds of structural builds.
